Our Certificate & Online Programs For 2022-2025

Our Certificate & Online Programs

A certificate program is a specialized education designed to develop or strengthen specific career skills. A certificate program is considered continuing education in some cases because it builds on your knowledge of particular career or industry. In other cases, it requires no prior education and can helpyou learn about a subject, career, or industry to pursue related jobs.


In this article, we’ll go over the differences between certificate programs and degrees, the benefits of completing a certificate program, and how you can determine which certificate program is best for you.


Thanks to the expansion of online education and the growing demand for skilled employees, there are now myriad certificate programs available. Whether you’re interested in from a storied college or university or a leading company, you have many options to consider.


Certificates tend to be divided into two levels:

  • Beginner programs are ideal for recent graduates who want to develop their skill set in a particular subject or industry—or career switchers interested in pivoting to new roles or industries who need a foundational understanding. For example, you can earn a Professional Certificate in social media marketing from Meta on Coursera.

  • Advanced programs are ideal for those looking to move ahead in their career, but who may benefit from fortifying important skills necessary to progress in their chosen career or industry. For example,you can learn advanced data processes using the database analysis language SQL.

How long does a certificate program take?

The length of each certificate program depends on what you study, but take far less time than a degree because they tend to prioritize practicality and job-ready skills. On average, certificate programs take around three to seven months, depending on what you’re learning and how the program is structured.
